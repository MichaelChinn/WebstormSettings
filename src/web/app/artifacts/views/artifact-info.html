Artifact Page Information

An artifact is an item proving a desired instructional activity or a description of a non-observable, yet a desirable professional practice.

The artifact page gives you space to collect your artifacts during the school year. Artifacts you've uploaded or collected through your smartphone would appear here.

This is an organization stage. When you click the "Evidence" button you'll be guided through the process of making an Artifact into a compliant Evidence item.

A few points to keep in mind:
  • You don't have to submit every Artifact you have collected.
  • Artifacts you have collected here are private until you submit them as Evidence.
  • At the end of the year, all artifacts that are not submitted as Evidence will be deleted.
